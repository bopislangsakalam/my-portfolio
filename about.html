About Me

I'm Devon Angelo M. Ceynas, a 18-year-old student from the Philippines, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology major in Web Technology at the University of the Cordilleras.

I’m deeply interested in creating modern, user-centered websites and digital experiences that blend both creativity and functionality. I enjoy exploring front-end development, design principles, and how technology can be used to communicate ideas in impactful ways. My goal is to grow as a web developer who builds projects that inspire and connect people through design and innovation.
